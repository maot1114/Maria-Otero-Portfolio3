About

I believe the best solutions happen when creativity, technology, and empathy come together to solve real problems.

I recently earned a Bachelor of Science in Creative Technology & Design from the University of Colorado Boulder. My work focuses on creating thoughtful digital and physical experiences by combining user-centered design, emerging technologies, and practical problem-solving.

Through my studies, I built experience in UX/UI design, interaction design, motion design, product development, prototyping, digital fabrication, and emerging technologies. Working across these disciplines taught me how to approach challenges from multiple perspectives and transform ideas into intuitive, impactful solutions.

Before transitioning into design, I spent more than eight years working in veterinary medicine, including veterinary cardiology. That experience strengthened my empathy, communication, attention to detail, and ability to collaborate with multidisciplinary teams while identifying opportunities to improve everyday experiences.

Today, I'm excited to apply my design skills to create products and experiences that are intuitive, accessible, and meaningful for the people who use them.
